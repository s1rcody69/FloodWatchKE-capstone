About FloodWatch Kenya

OUR STORY

Every year, Kenyans lose their lives, homes and livelihoods to floods that could have been survived with just a little more warning. In 2026 alone, over 66 people were killed and more than 50,000 were displaced across 17 counties — leaving thousands of families with nothing overnight. The tragedy is not just the floods themselves, but the silence before them.

FloodWatch Kenya was built to break that silence. We are a community-powered early warning platform that puts life-saving flood information directly in the hands of every Kenyan with a smartphone. No government infrastructure needed. No expensive equipment. Just neighbours looking out for neighbours — reporting, confirming, and sharing alerts in real time so that no family is ever caught off guard again.
