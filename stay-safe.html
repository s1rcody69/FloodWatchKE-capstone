Flood Safety Guide

STAY SAFE

Whether a flood is coming, happening right now, or just passed — this guide tells you exactly what to do to keep yourself and your family safe.

In immediate danger?

Don't read guides — call 999 or 112 right now.

BEFORE A FLOOD

Store Important Documents

Keep ID, birth certificates and insurance papers in a waterproof bag ready to grab.

Know Your Evacuation Route

Identify the nearest high ground and practice the route with your family

Save Emergency Contacts

Save 999, 112, Kenya Red Cross 1199 and a trusted neighbour in your phone

Pack an Emergency Bag

Water, food, medicine, torch, phone charger — enough for 3 days

Follow FloodWatch Alerts

Check FloodWatch Kenya regularly during rainy season for reports in your area

DURING A FLOOD

Move to Higher Ground

Leave immediately — don't wait to see how bad it gets. Take your emergency bag

Never Walk in Flood Water

Just 15cm of moving water can knock you off your feet. Find another route

Avoid Electrical Equipment

Don't touch switches or appliances if water is near. Cut power at the mains if safe

Call For Help if Trapped

Call 999 or 112 immediately. Stay on the line and tell them your exact location

Help Your Neighbours

Check on elderly or disabled neighbours — they may not be able to evacuate alone

AFTER A FLOOD

Don't Return Too Soon

Wait until authorities confirm it is safe. Flood water weakens foundations and walls

Avoid Flood Water

Flood water is contaminated with sewage and chemicals. Do not touch it or let children near it

Document All Damage

Take photos of everything before cleaning up — you'll need this for insurance or relief claims

Check For Structural Damage

Look for cracks in walls, floors and ceilings before entering any building

Seek Medical Help if Needed

If you were exposed to flood water seek medical attention — waterborne diseases spread fast

Evacuation Checklist

Grab these before you leave

National ID / Passport
Phone + Charger
Drinking Water (3 days)
Non-perishable Food
Prescription Medicine
First Aid Kit
Torch + Batteries
Warm Clothing
Cash (small notes)
Baby Supplies (if needed)
Important Documents
Whistle (to signal for help)
