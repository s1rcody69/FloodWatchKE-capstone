How FloodWatch Kenya Works

📍

Step 1: Report

A resident spots flooding and submits a report with their location, a description, and the severity level.

✅

Step 2: Confirm

Neighbours in the same area confirm the report. More confirmations automatically raise the severity level.

🔔

Step 3: Stay Safe

Others see the alert, check the Stay Safe guide, and access emergency contacts to protect their families.
