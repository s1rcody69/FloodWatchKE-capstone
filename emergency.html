What To Tell Emergency Services

When you call, speak clearly and give them these four things first.

01

📍 Your Exact Location

Name your estate, nearest landmark, road name and county

02

🌊 Describe the Emergency

Water level, number of people trapped, any injuries

03

👥 Number of People

How many adults, children and elderly need help

04

📱Stay On The Line

Don't hang up — keep talking until help arrives
